## Hi, I'm Geroen Joris

Cloud Architect, Security Professional, and Musician.

### My Background

I specialize in **AWS cloud architecture**, **DevSecOps**, and **software security**. With years of experience building secure, scalable cloud solutions, I help organizations modernize and secure their infrastructure.

### What I Do

- **Cloud Architecture**: Designing and implementing AWS solutions that are scalable, cost-effective, and secure
- **DevSecOps**: Integrating security into every phase of the development lifecycle
- **Security**: Securing applications and infrastructure following best practices and compliance requirements
- **Mentoring**: Sharing knowledge and experience with teams and the tech community

### Beyond Tech

When I'm not working on cloud architecture, I play the alto saxophone. This passion for music has inspired me to build projects that combine technology and art, such as [Fasleutel](https://gjoris.github.io/fasleutel/), an interactive music note quiz.
